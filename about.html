THE NATURE POSITIVE INITIATIVE

Promoting the integrity and implementation of Nature Positive by 2030

Learn More
"Nature positive is not a slogan – it is an ambitious goal and should not be used to imply something is green or nature-friendly. It refers to measurable outcomes that contribute to halting and reversing nature loss with significant benefits to society."

Our Mission

The Nature Positive Initiative is the second phase of work that commenced in 2019, with the development of nature positive by 2030 as the global goal for nature – its equivalent to the 1.5C goal that exists for climate.

Core Areas Description Key Objectives
Integrity Preservation Preserving the integrity of 'Nature Positive' as a measurable 2030 global goal Establish clear metrics and guidelines for measurement
Stakeholder Engagement Coordinating active engagement across business, government, and other stakeholders Build inclusive partnerships and coalitions
Implementation Support Providing tools and guidance for nature positive outcomes Develop practical frameworks and resources
Framework Implementation Supporting the Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework Monitor and report on implementation progress
